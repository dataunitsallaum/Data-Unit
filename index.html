Data governance — classification

Clear rules for what data may be used with which AI tools.

🔴 Confidential

PII, financial accounts, unpublished contracts, health & legal data. Never in external AI — internal AI with access controls only.

🟠 Restricted

Internal KPIs, client names, vessel schedules, pricing, HR. Approved internal AI only — no public copy-paste.

🟡 Internal

Aggregated reports, summaries, anonymized trends, process docs. Usable with AI after removing identifiers.

🟢 Public

Published reports, public company info, industry data. Freely usable with any AI tool.
